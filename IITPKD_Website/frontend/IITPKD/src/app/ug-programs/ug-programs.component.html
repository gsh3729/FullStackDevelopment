Current UG Programs 

  IIT Palakkad offers four-year B.Tech programme in four branches: Civil Engineering, Computer Science
  and Engineering, Electrical Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. The yearly intake is about 120
  students, equally distributed in all four branches. Students have come from almost all parts of the
  country, providing a truly cosmopolitan and multicultural environment.

Offered B.Tech Courses

Civil Engineering 30 seats
Computer Science & Engineering 50 seats
Electrical Engineering 50 seats
Mechanical Engineering 30 seats
